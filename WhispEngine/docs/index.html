Description

Empty Universe, where everything happens

Empty Universe is an exhaustic adventure where you will have to defend the ship of enemies. You will have three types of astronauts in your control. Each astronaut has his own job in order to get all ship needs covered.

Survive five days and nights to win. Don't let the enemies kill your astronauts and manage your resources well.

  • Recollector

    Will catch stars in main platform and bring to storages in order to get more and upgrade the ship and reclute new agents

  • Chef

    His mission is to cook food and put it on tables in order to feed the agents when they are tired

  • Defender

    He will patrol around the ship and will run when enemy comes to defend. Once enemy killed, all defenders will go to a equitative point of the patrol in favor of being closer in other enemy surprise attack

  • Star Atractor

    It will atract stars. The more you update it the more stars it will attract and more quickly

  • Storages

    Star dust needs to be saved in storages

  • Enemy

    Enemies will attack one by one our ship, each day will go faster and do more damage
